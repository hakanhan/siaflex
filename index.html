FRAMEWORK 03 OF 06

Continuous Threat
Exposure Management

Gartner CTEM Framework · 5 Phases · Perpetual Cycle

Replaces annual point-in-time security assessments with a continuous cycle. Real risk prioritization using EPSS exploit prediction and CISA KEV active threat data.

1
Scoping
IAS-01
Define what to protect. Asset inventory, attack surface identification.
2
Discovery
VRM-01/02
Find all vulnerabilities in scope. External, internal, web applications.
3
Prioritization
EPSS + KEV
CVSS alone is insufficient. Exploit prediction and active threat data for real risk scoring.
4
Validation
VRM-05
Nuclei testing eliminates false positives. Theoretical vs actual exploitable risk.
5
Mobilization
VRM-03
Validated findings become actions. Remediation tracking and closure verification.
KEY DIFFERENTIATION
CTEM transforms security from a project into a continuous process. Instead of an annual report — continuous visibility, risk-based prioritization and closure tracking. Siaflex delivers each phase as a standalone service, or the complete cycle under the SVC-CTEM umbrella package.
